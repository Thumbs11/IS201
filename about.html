About Me

This is what I do.

I grew up in Utah. I grew up in the Salt Lake valley and attended Olympus Highschool.

I currently attend Brigham Young University and I am business Major. I also work full time for one of the fastest growing start-up companies in the state of Utah, The Grit Marketing.

Family is an extremely important part of my life. I grew up in a family of five. My mother is the Dean of Nursing at Gaylen College of Nursing, and my Dad does wealth management and financial consulting. My parents taught be the important lesson that nothing is given in life and that if you want something, you need to work for it. I am also set to get married this coming October and I am extremely grateful for my fiánce, she has shaped the person I am.

My Faith is an extremely huge part of who I am. I served a two year LDS mission in San Antionio, Texas. On my mission I learned amazing communication skills, planning skills, and leadership skills that I couldn't have gotten anywhere else. I also learned the Spanish Language and am currently fluent in it.

I have a lot of different hobbies. As often as possible, you will find me on the golf course competing with friends and family. I love to Hunt and go Deer and Elk hunting each year. I love basketball and play pickup basketball and the rec center most nights. I also love UTVing in the Sand Dunes in Southern Utah. I am also an avid sports fan and you will find me at any sporting event that I can go to.
